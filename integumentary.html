The Integumentary System



  • Made up of your skin, which is the largest organ in the body
  • Function of the system is to protect internal organs and vessles, water balance, tempurature regulation, waste disposal receptor organs, blood reservoir, and vitamin D production


    • The skin has 3 layers
  • Epidermis - Outermost layer, has no blood vessles, complete reset every 35-45 days
  • Dermis - Middle region, has blood vessles, capillaries, lymphatic vessels, nerves, hair, sweat glands
  • Hypodermis - deepest region
