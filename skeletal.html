The Skeletal System

  • The skeletal system is made up of 206 bones and connective flexible cartillage
  • These help support your body structure
  • It also stores minerals like calcium
  • It produces blood cells in bone marrow
  • It stores energy in yellow marrow
  • The outside is compact bone, which is hard
  • The inside is spongy bone, which is soft



    • Divisions of the skeleton



    Axial Skeleton

  • Includes skull, vertebral column, and rib cage
  • The vertebral column consists of the cervical(7), thoracic(12), lumbar(5), sacrum, and coccyx divisions
  • All 33 vertebre are divided by cartilage



    • Appendicular Skeleton

  • Includes arms, legs, pelvis, and shoulder



    • Bone structures



  • Long bones - Humerus, femus, metacarpals, metatarsals, phalanges, ulna, radius, tibia, fibula
  • Short bones - carpals, tarsals
  • Flat bones - Crandial bones, sternium, scapula, ribs
  • Irregular bones - Vertebrae, some facial bones, pelvic bone
  • Sesamoid bones - patella
